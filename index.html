Abstract

[cite: 3] Conventional visual SLAM often fails under rapid motion or low illumination. Edged USLAM presents a hybrid visual-inertial system that extends Ultimate SLAM with an edge-aware frontend and a lightweight depth module[cite: 5]. The frontend enhances event frames for robust feature tracking, while the depth module improves motion compensation and scale consistency[cite: 6].

New Aerial Dataset

We introduce a new aerial dataset featuring diverse motion and illumination conditions using the TESTUDO platform[cite: 40, 46, 172].
